**The funnel is flat.** Every page dead-ends at "Let's Talk." No case study depth, no process transparency, no guides. Buyer journey: arrive → skim credentials → leave or call.

**The portfolio is a gallery, not a sales tool.** 27 project pages with no structured narrative.

## 3. Copywriting — Grade: C−

**Worst line:**
> "We take pride in seamlessly integrating our expertise with customized solutions to deliver extraordinary outcomes for our valued clients."

Every word is filler. Could be deleted entirely.

**Other offenders:**
- "Purposeful Work Done by Passionate People" — substitutable with any firm
- "125+ Years of Excellence" — the number is interesting, "Excellence" wastes it
- "Trusted partners in your success" — most common phrase in B2B professional services
- "Complete array of services" — catalog language

**What's genuinely strong:**
- "Meets clients where they are — then takes them further"
- "You're Looking to Build Something Great. So Are We."

**Most damning finding:** The Jeanette Vaughan testimonial — written by a client — outperforms every headline. When your client accidentally writes better copy than your brand team, the brief was wrong.

**Headline hierarchy: D+**

## Priority Recommendations

1. **Convert 5 portfolio projects into real case studies** — editorial fix, no redesign. 400–600 words each: challenge → decision → outcome → quote.
2. **Add one mid-funnel reciprocity offer per sector** — cost benchmarking guide, pre-design checklist. Gate with email.
3. **Put faces on the tagline** — one rotating homepage feature with a named person.
4. **Name the rankings, show client logos** — 20-minute edit with outsized trust impact.
5. **Reorient Insights toward client search intent** — 2 articles/month answering questions clients actually search.
