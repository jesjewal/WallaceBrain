# Resume & Cover Letter Tailoring Patterns

## Core Principle
Jesse is genuinely skilled across all domains. The tailoring is about choosing which skillset to put in front of a specific audience. Every version is true — the framing is strategic, not compensatory.

**Primary levers:** Title, profile summary, skills section order. Work experience stays mostly stable; bullet language shifts to surface relevant work.

---

## Resume Versions by Role Type

### AEC / Content / Creative (Shive-Hattery model)
- **Title:** Creative Strategist + Content Producer
- **Lead skills:** Creative & Production, InDesign, visual storytelling
- **Cover letter:** Lead with what you built; breadth across formats; AI practitioner

### Performance Marketing (TrueNorth model)
- **Title:** Digital Marketing + Content Leader
- **Lead skills:** Communications & Strategy → Web/Digital/Analytics → Creative
- **Cover letter:** Personal/cultural hook opener; deep company research; name gaps directly

### Instructional Design (UFG/GreenState model)
- **Title:** Instructional Design + Content Leader
- **Lead skills:** Instructional Design & Development first
- **Cover letter:** Academic lineage (Hemphill → Merrill); bespoke module as proof; system-level thinking

### Agency / Art Direction (Informatics model)
- **Title:** Art Director
- **Lead skills:** Brand identity, logo design, creative concepting
- **Cover letter:** Brand craft depth; Digital Stories as agency credential; AI fluency as explicit requirement match

---

## Reusable Cover Letter Blocks

**IDT system-level positioning (UFG + GreenState):**
"Somewhere along the way I stopped seeing individual courses and started seeing the whole system underneath them..."

**Gap acknowledgment formula:**
Name the gap directly → "here's what I have done" → "here's how fast I close gaps"

---

## What to Always Include (all roles)
- 20+ years framing (establishes seniority)
- AI practitioner credential (workflows and systems thinking, not casual use)
- Mentorship / team development
- Quick learner / growth mindset (genuine and proven)

---

## What to Leave Background for Creative/Design Roles
- Instructional design & LMS platforms
- Economic/workforce data analysis
- Grant writing
- Community & workforce development
