## Services

**Biological Safety Cabinet (BSC) Certification**
NSF 49 standards. Inflow/downflow velocity, HEPA filter integrity, performance validation after install, relocation, or maintenance.

**Laminar Flow Hood Certification**
Unidirectional airflow, filter integrity, ISO compliance for contamination-free environments.

**Isolator CAI/CACI Certification**
USP 797 + USP 800 standards. Pressure differential, airflow, HEPA efficiency, containment performance for hazardous and non-hazardous drug compounding.

**Cleanroom Certification**
ISO classifications + GMP requirements. Particle count testing, airflow/pressure differentials, HEPA integrity. Pharmaceutical, biotech, research.

**Fume Hood Testing**
Face velocity, smoke visualization, ANSI/ASHRAE + OSHA compliance. Personnel protection from chemical exposure.

**HEPA Filter Integrity Testing**
Aerosol challenge methods. Leak detection and filtration efficiency verification.

**Microbiological Testing Services**
Surface + air sampling, microbial ID, trending analysis. Reported via ISO-certified microbiology lab.

**Certification Documentation**
Compliance reports for USP 797/800, ISO 14644-1, ANEX 1, FDA guidelines.
